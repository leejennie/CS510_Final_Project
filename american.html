2. Tasty n Alder

Rating: 3883
$$ American

(503) 621-9251
580 SW 12th Ave
Downtown

Tasty n Alder, a John Gorham restaurant in Downtown Portland, Oregon, is a modern steakhouse featuring a fun, family-style menu of innovative dishes with diverse international origins. In Tasty n Alder’s welcoming open dining space that is both urban and comfortable, a guest’s experience can be as versatile as the menu itself with seating at the lively bar, outdoor seats for the downtown Portland experience, or at a classic dining table.
